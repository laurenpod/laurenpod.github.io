MINT CHOCOLATE CHIP ICE CREAM☞

here is how to make mint chocolate chip ice cream!!

this is what yours should look like!✓

★INGREDIENTS★

  • whole milk (cold, do not use whole milk)
  • sugar (granulated)
  • heavy cream (cold)
  • pure peppermint extract (you may also use vanilla)
  • semi-sweet chocolate bar

★AMOUNTS★

  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1.5-2 tsp peppermint extract (or to taste)
  • 4-oz semi sweet chocolate bar (chopped into tiny pieces)

★INSTRUCTIONS★

  1. take a bowl and a whisk
  2. use the whisk to combine the milk and sugar
  3. do this until the sugar is dissolved
  4. stir in the heavy cream
  5. stir in the peppermint
  6. turn on the ice cream maker
  7. pour the mixture in
  8. let it churn for around 25 minutes
  9. during the last five minutes of churning add the chocolate
  10. transfer ice cream in an airtight container
  11. leave it for at least 2-3 hours
